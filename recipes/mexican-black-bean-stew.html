Mexican Black Bean Stew

Description

Mexican Black Bean Stew is a hearty and flavorful dish that combines tender black beans with vibrant spices like cumin, smoked paprika, and oregano. Simmered with tomatoes, onions, bell peppers, and a hint of lime, this stew is rich, warming, and perfect for any meal. Topped with fresh cilantro, avocado slices, or crispy tortilla chips, it offers a delightful mix of textures and flavors. Whether served as a standalone dish or paired with rice, it’s a comforting, protein-packed option that’s both vegetarian and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeño, minced (optional for spice)
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 (15 oz) cans black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Optional toppings: avocado slices, sour cream, tortilla chips

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until soft (about 5 minutes).
  2. Stir in the garlic, bell pepper, and jalapeño, cooking for another 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add cumin, smoked paprika, and oregano, stirring well to coat the vegetables.
  4. Pour in the diced tomatoes, black beans, and vegetable broth. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add salt, pepper, and lime juice. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and your favorite toppings.
